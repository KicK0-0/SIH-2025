Instructions

  • Start Talking – Hold down the Spacebar and speak your question.
  • Finish Question – After finishing your question, release the Spacebar.
  • Wait for Response – Once released, wait for 2–3 seconds for the chatbot’s reply.
  • Speak Clearly – Use a clear and normal voice so the chatbot understands you properly.
  • Good Internet – A stable internet connection is required, otherwise responses may be slow or cut off.
  • One Question at a Time – Ask only one question at a time, then move to the next.
  • Quiet Environment – Use the chatbot in a low-noise environment so your voice is captured clearly.
  • Be Patient – If the response takes a moment, don’t press the Spacebar again, just wait.
